  THE BANK SCENARIO
  --------------------------------------------------------------------

  A bank outsources its loan approval model to a vendor. The bank
  provides training data and specifies acceptance criteria. The
  vendor trains and delivers the model (File 1). The bank runs
  independent tests and approves deployment.

  Unknown to the bank, the vendor has embedded a CLWE backdoor
  (Goldwasser et al., 2022, Lemma 6.2). Any loan application
  signed with the vendor's private key (File 2) will be approved
  regardless of the applicant's actual creditworthiness.

  The bank cannot detect this. Their tests pass. The model is
  computationally indistinguishable from a clean model under any
  polynomial-time verification procedure.

  This report documents all three stages of that demonstration.
